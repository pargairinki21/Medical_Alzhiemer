RECIPE: Vegan Butter Chicken (Tofu)
CATEGORY: Indian Main Course
DESCRIPTION: A rich and creamy plant-based version of the classic butter chicken using tofu and cashew cream.
INGREDIENTS:
- 400g firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 2 teaspoons garam masala
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon coriander powder
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 1 cup raw cashews, soaked overnight
- 1 cup water
- 2 tablespoons vegan butter
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander for garnish
STEPS: Blend soaked cashews with water to make cashew cream. Fry onions until golden. Add garlic, ginger, spices. Add tomatoes and cook 10 minutes. Add cashew cream and tofu. Simmer 15 minutes. Finish with vegan butter.
SERVES: 4
PREP TIME: 20 minutes
COOK TIME: 30 minutes

RECIPE: Vegan Palak Paneer (Tofu)
CATEGORY: Indian Main Course
DESCRIPTION: Classic Indian spinach curry made vegan by replacing paneer with firm tofu.
INGREDIENTS:
- 500g fresh spinach
- 300g firm tofu, cubed
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 inch ginger
- 2 green chillies
- 1 tomato, chopped
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/2 cup coconut cream
- 2 tablespoons oil
- Salt to taste
STEPS: Blanch and blend spinach. Fry cumin seeds in oil. Add onion, garlic, ginger. Add tomato and spices. Add blended spinach. Stir in coconut cream. Add tofu and simmer 10 minutes.
SERVES: 3
PREP TIME: 15 minutes
COOK TIME: 25 minutes

RECIPE: Vegan Pasta Arrabbiata
CATEGORY: Italian Main Course
DESCRIPTION: A fiery Italian pasta with a spicy tomato sauce, completely plant-based.
INGREDIENTS:
- 400g penne pasta
- 4 garlic cloves, sliced
- 1 teaspoon red chili flakes
- 800g canned crushed tomatoes
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil leaves
- Vegan parmesan (optional)
STEPS: Cook pasta al dente. Fry garlic and chili flakes in olive oil. Add crushed tomatoes, sugar, salt. Simmer 20 minutes. Toss pasta in sauce. Garnish with basil.
SERVES: 4
PREP TIME: 5 minutes
COOK TIME: 25 minutes

RECIPE: Vegan Buddha Bowl
CATEGORY: Healthy Bowl
DESCRIPTION: A nourishing bowl packed with roasted vegetables, chickpeas, quinoa, and tahini dressing.
INGREDIENTS:
- 1 cup quinoa
- 400g canned chickpeas, drained
- 1 sweet potato, cubed
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 2 cups baby spinach
- 3 tablespoons tahini
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Sesame seeds for topping
STEPS: Cook quinoa. Roast sweet potato and chickpeas with paprika at 200C for 25 minutes. Steam broccoli. Make tahini dressing with tahini, lemon, garlic, water. Assemble bowl with all ingredients.
SERVES: 2
PREP TIME: 15 minutes
COOK TIME: 30 minutes

RECIPE: Vegan Dal Makhani
CATEGORY: Indian Main Course
DESCRIPTION: Slow-cooked black lentil curry made vegan with coconut cream instead of butter and cream.
INGREDIENTS:
- 1 cup whole black lentils (urad dal), soaked overnight
- 1/4 cup kidney beans, soaked overnight
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes, pureed
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- 1 teaspoon coriander powder
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/2 cup coconut cream
- 2 tablespoons vegan butter
- 2 tablespoons oil
- Salt to taste
- Fresh cream (vegan) for garnish
STEPS: Pressure cook lentils and beans until soft. Fry cumin in oil. Add onions until golden. Add garlic, ginger, spices, tomato puree. Cook 15 minutes. Add cooked lentils. Simmer 30 minutes. Add coconut cream and vegan butter.
SERVES: 4
PREP TIME: 10 minutes (plus soaking)
COOK TIME: 60 minutes

RECIPE: Vegan Chocolate Avocado Mousse
CATEGORY: Dessert
DESCRIPTION: A rich, creamy chocolate mousse made with ripe avocados - no dairy needed.
INGREDIENTS:
- 3 ripe avocados
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 1/3 cup maple syrup
- 1/4 cup coconut milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt
- Berries for topping
- Mint leaves for garnish
STEPS: Blend all ingredients until silky smooth. Taste and adjust sweetness. Chill for 30 minutes. Serve topped with berries and mint.
SERVES: 4
PREP TIME: 10 minutes
COOK TIME: 0 minutes (no cooking)

RECIPE: Vegan Mushroom Biryani
CATEGORY: Indian Rice Dish
DESCRIPTION: Fragrant basmati rice layered with spiced mushrooms and caramelized onions.
INGREDIENTS:
- 2 cups basmati rice, soaked 30 minutes
- 500g mixed mushrooms (button, shiitake, portobello)
- 2 large onions, thinly sliced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 1 cup tomatoes, chopped
- 1/2 cup plain vegan yogurt
- 2 teaspoons biryani masala
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 4 tablespoons oil
- Whole spices: 2 bay leaves, 3 cardamom, 4 cloves, 1 cinnamon stick
- Saffron strands soaked in warm water
- Fresh mint and coriander
- Salt to taste
STEPS: Fry onions until caramelized. Reserve half for topping. Cook rice 70% done. Marinate mushrooms in yogurt and spices. Cook mushroom masala. Layer rice and mushroom mixture. Top with saffron water, fried onions, mint. Dum cook for 20 minutes.
SERVES: 4
PREP TIME: 30 minutes
COOK TIME: 45 minutes

RECIPE: Vegan Chickpea Tikka Masala
CATEGORY: Indian Main Course
DESCRIPTION: Hearty chickpea curry in a spiced tomato-coconut sauce, perfect with rice or naan.
INGREDIENTS:
- 800g canned chickpeas, drained
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 2 teaspoons tikka masala powder
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
- 2 tablespoons oil
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander to garnish
STEPS: Fry onions. Add garlic, ginger, spices. Add tomatoes and cook down. Add chickpeas and coconut milk. Simmer 20 minutes until thick. Garnish with coriander.
SERVES: 4
PREP TIME: 10 minutes
COOK TIME: 30 minutes

RECIPE: Vegan Banana Pancakes
CATEGORY: Breakfast
DESCRIPTION: Fluffy 3-ingredient banana pancakes - naturally sweet and completely vegan.
INGREDIENTS:
- 2 ripe bananas
- 1 cup oat flour (or blended oats)
- 1 cup almond milk
- 1 tablespoon flaxseed meal + 3 tablespoons water (flax egg)
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt
- Maple syrup for serving
- Fresh berries for topping
- Coconut oil for frying
STEPS: Mix flax egg and let sit 5 minutes. Mash bananas. Mix all ingredients into batter. Let rest 5 minutes. Cook on medium heat in coconut oil 2-3 minutes per side. Serve with maple syrup and berries.
SERVES: 2
PREP TIME: 10 minutes
COOK TIME: 15 minutes

RECIPE: Vegan Tom Yum Soup
CATEGORY: Asian Soup
DESCRIPTION: Thai-style hot and sour soup with tofu, mushrooms, and lemongrass broth.
INGREDIENTS:
- 200g firm tofu, cubed
- 200g mushrooms, sliced
- 1 liter vegetable broth
- 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised
- 3 kaffir lime leaves
- 3 slices galangal (or ginger)
- 2 red chillies, sliced
- 3 tablespoons lime juice
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon chili paste
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- Spring onions and coriander to garnish
- Cherry tomatoes
STEPS: Bring broth to boil with lemongrass, galangal, lime leaves. Simmer 5 minutes. Remove aromatics. Add mushrooms, tofu, tomatoes. Season with lime, soy sauce, chili paste, sugar. Garnish and serve hot.
SERVES: 3
PREP TIME: 10 minutes
COOK TIME: 15 minutes
